About Me

A Middle Tennessee native, I graduated from MTSU in 2018 with a BS in Computer Science. At MTSU, I studied topics such as algorithms and data structures, neural networks and machine learning, compiler design and development, database management systems, and the general theory of programming languages. In addition, I also studied and developed with languages such as C, C++, C#, F#, Python, SQL, TSQL/MSSQL, and Tiger. My minor was in Mathematics, and studied subjects such as Calculus & Analytic Geometrey, Linear Algebra, and Calc Based Probability & Statistics. On-campus activities included MTSU's chapter of the Association For Computing Machinery,and the Computer Science Connections club.

Currently, I am enrolled in Vanderbilt University's Coding Bootcamp to continue my education with a solid foundation in web development, and real world styled projects, utilizing HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, jQuey, Node.js, Express.js, Passport.js, MongoDB, MySQL, and React, amongst other technologies.
